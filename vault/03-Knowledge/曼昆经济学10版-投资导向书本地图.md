# 曼昆经济学10版-投资导向书本地图

## 你的阅读策略
- 目标不是“从头到尾读完”，而是先建立投资判断骨架。
- 路线：先必读 A 线，再按需求补 B 线，最后查阅 C 线。

## A 线（必读，先建立投资判断语言）
- Chapter 1: Ten Principles of Economics
- Chapter 2: Thinking Like an Economist
- Chapter 3: Interdependence and the Gains from Trade
- Chapter 4: The Market Forces of Supply and Demand
- Chapter 5: Elasticity and Its Application
- Chapter 6: Supply, Demand, and Government Policies
- Chapter 27: Saving, Investment, and the Financial System
- Chapter 28: The Basic Tools of Finance
- Chapter 30: The Monetary System
- Chapter 31: Money Growth and Inflation
- Chapter 34: Aggregate Demand and Aggregate Supply
- Chapter 35: The Influence of Monetary and Fiscal Policy on Aggregate Demand

## B 线（进阶，建立行业与政策理解）
- Chapter 7-9: Markets and Welfare / Taxation / International Trade
- Chapter 10-13: Externalities / Public Goods / Healthcare / Tax System
- Chapter 29: Unemployment
- Chapter 32-33: Open-Economy Macroeconomics
- Chapter 36-37: Inflation-Unemployment trade-off / Macro policy debates

## C 线（查阅，按你遇到的问题回看）
- Chapter 14-23: Firm behavior, market structure, labor, inequality, advanced micro topics
- Chapter 24-26: Macro data and long-run growth foundations
- Chapter 38: How Economists Use Data

## 你每轮都要回答的 4 个投资问题
1. 这个概念改变了我对“风险-收益”的哪一条判断？
2. 这个概念在现实市场中最容易被误用在哪里？
3. 如果这个概念失效，通常会是什么前提被破坏？
4. 这个概念对应我投资流程中的哪一步（选资产/定仓位/控风险）？
